My favorite band of all time is Gojira. They're a metal band from France.

  • They started in 1996
  • Their band name was Godzilla originally.
  • In 2001, they changed their name to Gojira due to copyright reasons
  • They released their first album in 2001, which was called Terra Incognita.
  • It was their third and fourth albums, From Mars to Sirius (2003) and The Way of All Flesh (2008) respectively, that brought them into the spotlight.
  • They were signed onto a major record label called Roadrunner Records in 2011, which allowed their albums to gain more visibility.
  • They were nominated for a Grammy in 2016 with their song Silvera.
  • They performed at the Olympics in 2024, making them the first metal band to ever perform at the Olympics.
  • They also won a Grammy for their performance at the Olympics
