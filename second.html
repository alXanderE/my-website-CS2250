Here are some really cool games


Team Fortress 2


Team Fortress 2 is a really cool game. It's a shooter game developed by Valve, the same company that made Steam!.
The game has two teams that fight against each other, RED and BLU. There are nine different character to choose from.
The nine classes are the Scout, Soldier, Pyro, Demoman, Heavy, Engineer, Medic, Sniper, and Spy.
This game is very fun and wacky. The slapstick humor and goofiness is what makes this game truly timeless.

Fallout: New Vegas


Fallout: New Vegas is a really cool and dark game. It's an RPG developed by Obsidian Entertainment.
It's first person and takes place in a post-apocalyptic Earth. The game's location is Las Vegas, or New Vegas as it's called in-game
It's known for it's grittiness and choice-based story on how your character starts off as a simple Courier but evolves into a leader and powerful person.

Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is a really cool and dark game. It's an RPG developed by CD Projekt Red.
It's first person and takes place in the dark futuristic world of Cyberpunk. Your character is a merc in Night City.
During a job, the main protagonist gets caught in a bad situation that you need to solve or else you'll die.
This game is known for it's dark and depressing story. It's also choice-based which is really cool.

Persona 4 Golden

Persona 4 Golden is my favorite game of the Persona series. It's very cool and has an engaging story.
I once played this game for 8 hours a day straight for two weeks. That's when I finished it.
It's a JRPG developed by Atlus. It takes place in a small town known as Inaba. Your character is a high schooler
moving to a new town for a year. Weird things start happening in the town of Inaba, and the main character and his friends
get caught up in it. It's a very cool games that you should play.
